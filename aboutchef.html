About Chef

Chef Javier

Chef Javier Haygood, affectionately known as "JT" or "Chef" left his job as a computer programmer at a national company in 2005 to pursue his passion for cooking. Self-taught, JT honed his craft to become one of the most celebrated chefs in Alabama. He has worked at several five-star restaurants in the greater Birmingham, AL area, most notably, as the Executive Chef at the City Club of Birmingham and Hoover Country Club.
